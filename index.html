How to Play

  1. Start the Game: Enter a number between 1 and 10 in the input box.
  2. Submit Your Guess: Click the "Check" button to see if your guess matches the randomly generated number.
  3. Check the Result: You’ll get a message indicating whether you guessed correctly or not, and your score will be updated accordingly.
  4. Try Again: Keep guessing until you either win or your score runs out!
